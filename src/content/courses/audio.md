## Coming Soon

Shape sound from recording to release. Audio production covers microphone technique, DAW workflows, mixing, mastering, and publishing — whether you're making music, podcasts, or sound design.

This course builds from recording fundamentals through mixing techniques to mastering and distribution across platforms.

## Top Resources to Get Started

### Free & Open Source Tools
- [REAPER](https://www.reaper.fm/) — Professional DAW with unlimited free evaluation — used in film scoring, game audio, and music production
- [Audacity](https://www.audacityteam.org/) — Free, open-source multi-track audio editor for recording, editing, and basic mixing
- [Ardour](https://ardour.org/) — Free, open-source DAW for recording, editing, and mixing — full-featured professional workflow on Linux, macOS, and Windows
- [LMMS](https://lmms.io/) — Free, open-source DAW focused on beat-making and electronic music with built-in instruments and effects
- [GarageBand](https://www.apple.com/garageband/) — Free on Mac/iOS with loops, virtual instruments, and an intuitive interface for beginners
- [Cakewalk by BandLab](https://www.bandlab.com/products/cakewalk) — Formerly premium DAW, now completely free — unlimited tracks, VST support, and professional mixing tools (Windows)
- [Surge XT](https://surge-synthesizer.github.io/) — Open-source hybrid synthesizer with wavetable, FM, and subtractive synthesis — rivals commercial synths
- [Vital](https://vital.audio/) — Free spectral warping wavetable synthesizer matching the quality of Serum and Massive
- [Dexed](https://asb2m10.github.io/dexed/) — Open-source DX7 emulation that loads original presets — perfect for learning FM synthesis

### Interactive Courses & Structured Tutorials
- [Coursera — Music Production Specialization](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/music-production) — Berklee College of Music's structured program — audit for free
- [SoundGym School](https://www.soundgym.co/site/school) — Free audio learning programs curated by sound experts with ear training exercises
- [Waves — From Demo to Master](https://www.waves.com/courses/demo-to-master) — Free mixing and production course with Grammy-winning producer Dave Darlington
- [Recording Revolution](https://www.recordingrevolution.com/) — Home studio recording and mixing techniques with structured learning paths
- [Class Central — Audio Mixing](https://www.classcentral.com/subject/audio-mixing) — Aggregator of 400+ free audio mixing courses from universities and platforms
- [Mixing Music Live](https://www.mixingmusiclive.com/) — Online courses in live sound and music production
