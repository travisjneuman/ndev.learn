## Coming Soon

Shape sound from recording to release. Audio production covers microphone technique, DAW workflows, mixing, mastering, and publishing — whether you're making music, podcasts, or sound design.

This course builds from recording fundamentals through mixing techniques to mastering and distribution across platforms.

## Start Here — Learning Roadmap

A suggested path from zero to polished audio production. Follow these steps in order:

1. **Understand sound fundamentals** — Learn what frequency, amplitude, phase, and waveforms are; understand how decibels, sample rate, and bit depth work
2. **Set up your DAW** — Install REAPER or Audacity and learn navigation, recording, track management, and basic editing (cut, copy, fade)
3. **Learn microphone technique** — Understand polar patterns (cardioid, omni, figure-8), proximity effect, gain staging, and proper mic placement for vocals and instruments
4. **Record your first tracks** — Practice recording vocals or an instrument with clean gain, minimal background noise, and proper monitoring
5. **Study EQ and compression** — The two most important mixing tools; learn to cut problem frequencies, shape tone, and control dynamics
6. **Build a mixing workflow** — Gain staging, bussing, panning, aux sends, and effects chains; learn to balance a full mix from drums to vocals
7. **Add time-based effects** — Reverb, delay, and modulation effects; understand sends vs inserts and how to create depth and space
8. **Practice mastering basics** — Learn limiting, stereo imaging, loudness standards (LUFS), and how to prepare a final mix for distribution
9. **Explore sound design and synthesis** — Subtractive, FM, wavetable, and granular synthesis; build patches from scratch in Vital or Surge XT
10. **Publish and distribute** — Export formats (WAV, FLAC, MP3), metadata tagging, distribution platforms (DistroKid, Bandcamp), and podcast hosting

## Free & Open Source Tools

### DAWs & Editors

- [REAPER](https://www.reaper.fm/) — Professional DAW with unlimited free evaluation — used in film scoring, game audio, and music production (All levels)
- [Audacity](https://www.audacityteam.org/) — Free, open-source multi-track audio editor for recording, editing, and basic mixing (Beginner)
- [Ardour](https://ardour.org/) — Free, open-source DAW for recording, editing, and mixing — full-featured professional workflow on Linux, macOS, and Windows (Intermediate)
- [LMMS](https://lmms.io/) — Free, open-source DAW focused on beat-making and electronic music with built-in instruments and effects (Beginner)
- [Cakewalk by BandLab](https://www.bandlab.com/products/cakewalk) — Formerly premium DAW, now completely free — unlimited tracks, VST support, and professional mixing tools (Intermediate)
- [GarageBand](https://www.apple.com/garageband/) — Free on Mac/iOS with loops, virtual instruments, and an intuitive interface for beginners (Beginner)

### Free Synthesizers & Instruments

- [Vital](https://vital.audio/) — Free spectral warping wavetable synthesizer matching the quality of Serum and Massive — hundreds of YouTube tutorials available (Beginner to Advanced)
- [Surge XT](https://surge-synthesizer.github.io/) — Open-source hybrid synthesizer with wavetable, FM, and subtractive synthesis — rivals commercial synths (Intermediate to Advanced)
- [Dexed](https://asb2m10.github.io/dexed/) — Open-source DX7 emulation that loads original presets — perfect for learning FM synthesis (Intermediate)
- [Spitfire Audio LABS](https://labs.spitfireaudio.com/) — Free orchestral, piano, and textural instruments from a leading sample library company (Beginner)
- [Zebralette 3](https://u-he.com/products/zebralette/) — Free wavetable synth from u-he that grows with you from basic patches to complex sound design (Beginner to Intermediate)
- [Helm](https://tytel.org/helm/) — Open-source polyphonic synthesizer with a clean visual interface (Beginner)

### Free Effects Plugins

- [TDR Nova](https://www.tokyodawn.net/tdr-nova/) — Free dynamic equalizer — one of the best EQ plugins available at any price (Intermediate)
- [Valhalla Supermassive](https://valhalladsp.com/shop/reverb/valhalla-supermassive/) — Free delay and reverb plugin with lush, massive soundscapes (Beginner to Intermediate)
- [Kilohearts Essentials](https://kilohearts.com/products/kilohearts_essentials) — Free bundle of effects including EQ, compressor, delay, and distortion (Beginner)
