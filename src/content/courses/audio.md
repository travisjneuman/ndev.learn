## Coming Soon

Shape sound from recording to release. Audio production covers microphone technique, DAW workflows, mixing, mastering, and publishing — whether you're making music, podcasts, or sound design.

This course builds from recording fundamentals through mixing techniques to mastering and distribution across platforms.

## Top Resources to Get Started

### Free Software
- [REAPER](https://www.reaper.fm/) — Professional DAW with a free evaluation license
- [Audacity](https://www.audacityteam.org/) — Free, open-source audio editor
- [GarageBand](https://www.apple.com/garageband/) — Free on Mac/iOS, great for beginners
- [Cakewalk by BandLab](https://www.bandlab.com/products/cakewalk) — Free full-featured DAW for Windows

### Interactive Courses & Tutorials
- [Produce Like A Pro](https://www.youtube.com/c/ProduceLikeAPro) — Warren Huart's mixing and recording tutorials
- [Recording Revolution](https://www.recordingrevolution.com/) — Home studio recording techniques
- [Pensado's Place](https://www.youtube.com/c/PensadosPlace) — Interviews and masterclasses with top engineers
- [Coursera — Music Production Specialization](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/music-production) — Berklee College of Music

### Audio Engineering Channels
- [In The Mix](https://www.youtube.com/c/InTheMix) — Clear mixing and mastering tutorials
- [Musician on a Mission](https://www.youtube.com/c/Musicianonamission) — Home studio mixing guides
- [Dan Worrall](https://www.youtube.com/c/DanWorrall) — Deep technical audio engineering
- [Podcastage](https://www.youtube.com/c/Podcastage) — Microphone reviews and podcasting setup

### Books & References
- [Mixing Secrets for the Small Studio](https://www.cambridge-mt.com/ms/) — Mike Senior's practical mixing guide
- [The Recording Engineer's Handbook](https://www.amazon.com/Recording-Engineers-Handbook-Bobby-Owsinski/dp/1285442016) — Studio recording techniques
- [Mastering Audio](https://www.amazon.com/Mastering-Audio-Science-Bob-Katz/dp/0240818962) — Bob Katz's mastering bible
