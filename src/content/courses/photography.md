## Coming Soon

See the world through a creative lens. Photography and design cover composition, lighting, post-processing, and visual communication — from capturing images to creating polished graphic designs.

This course builds from camera fundamentals through Lightroom editing to Photoshop compositing and graphic design principles.

## Top Resources to Get Started

### Free & Open Source Tools
- [darktable](https://www.darktable.org/) — Professional RAW processor and photo workflow manager — the leading open-source Lightroom alternative with GPU acceleration
- [GIMP](https://www.gimp.org/) — Full-featured raster image editor with layers, masks, filters, and scripting — the closest free Photoshop replacement (v3.0 adds non-destructive editing)
- [RawTherapee](https://rawtherapee.com/) — Powerful RAW photo processor with batch processing and an approachable interface
- [Krita](https://krita.org/) — Open-source digital painting and illustration tool with professional brushes, layers, and color management
- [Inkscape](https://inkscape.org/) — Free vector graphics editor for logos, icons, illustrations, and scalable designs — open-source Illustrator alternative
- [digiKam](https://www.digikam.org/) — Open-source photo management with face recognition, geotagging, and batch editing across large libraries
- [Figma](https://www.figma.com/) — Free browser-based design tool for UI/UX, prototyping, and collaboration
- [Canva](https://www.canva.com/) — Accessible graphic design platform with templates for social media, presentations, and print

### Interactive Courses & Structured Tutorials
- [r/photoclass](https://www.reddit.com/r/photoclass/) — Reddit's free structured photography course covering fundamentals through advanced technique
- [Cambridge in Colour](https://www.cambridgeincolour.com/tutorials.htm) — In-depth technical photography tutorials on exposure, color, and digital imaging
- [Adorama — Exploring Photography](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLbFYEOX4haGp2vSKJCBQx7KF_SuVHECh8) — 50+ hours of free video lessons covering camera basics through advanced techniques
- [Coursera — Photography Basics and Beyond](https://www.coursera.org/courses?query=photography) — University-level photography courses — audit for free
- [Strobist](https://strobist.blogspot.com/) — The definitive free resource for learning off-camera flash and lighting control
- [Hack Design](https://hackdesign.org/) — 50 free design lessons curated by working designers, delivered weekly
- [PetaPixel — Free Photography Courses](https://petapixel.com/best-free-online-photography-courses/) — Curated list of the best free courses from top platforms
- [Photzy](https://photzy.com/) — Free photography eBooks and quick guides on composition, exposure, and post-processing

### Books & Long-Form References
- [Understanding Exposure](https://www.amazon.com/Understanding-Exposure-Fourth-Photographs-Camera/dp/1607748509) — Bryan Peterson's classic on aperture, shutter speed, and ISO — the foundation of photography
- [The Photographer's Eye (Remastered)](https://www.amazon.com/Photographers-Eye-Composition-Design-Digital/dp/0240809343) — Michael Freeman's essential guide to visual composition, updated with modern techniques
- [Understanding Composition](https://www.amazon.com/Bryan-Petersons-Understanding-Composition-Photographs/dp/0770433073) — Bryan Peterson on arranging visual elements for maximum impact
- [Picture This: How Pictures Work](https://www.amazon.com/Picture-This-How-Pictures-Work/dp/1587170302) — Molly Bang uses basic shapes to explain why composition works — surprisingly deep
- [Steal Like an Artist](https://austinkleon.com/steal/) — Austin Kleon on creative inspiration, influence, and finding your artistic voice
- [Refactoring UI](https://www.refactoringui.com/) — Practical design principles for developers — color, typography, spacing, and hierarchy

### Community, Inspiration & Practice
- [r/photography](https://www.reddit.com/r/photography/) — Large Reddit community for technique discussion, gear questions, and industry news
- [r/photocritique](https://www.reddit.com/r/photocritique/) — Submit your photos for constructive feedback from other photographers
- [Flickr](https://www.flickr.com/) — Long-running photo-sharing platform with active groups for every genre and technique
- [500px](https://500px.com/) — Curated photography community that rewards high-quality work with homepage features and contests ($150-$500 prizes)
- [DPReview Forums](https://www.dpreview.com/forums/) — In-depth discussions on digital photography, gear, and technique
- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/) — Free high-resolution photos for inspiration and study — see what works in real-world compositions
- [Dribbble](https://dribbble.com/) — Design community for graphic design, branding, and UI inspiration from working professionals
