## Coming Soon

See the world through a creative lens. Photography and design cover composition, lighting, post-processing, and visual communication — from capturing images to creating polished graphic designs.

This course builds from camera fundamentals through Lightroom editing to Photoshop compositing and graphic design principles.

## Top Resources to Get Started

### Free Software
- [darktable](https://www.darktable.org/) — Free, open-source Lightroom alternative
- [GIMP](https://www.gimp.org/) — Free, open-source Photoshop alternative
- [RawTherapee](https://rawtherapee.com/) — Free RAW photo processor
- [Figma](https://www.figma.com/) — Free browser-based design tool
- [Canva](https://www.canva.com/) — Easy graphic design for non-designers

### Interactive Courses & Tutorials
- [r/photoclass](https://www.reddit.com/r/photoclass/) — Reddit's free photography course
- [Cambridge in Colour](https://www.cambridgeincolour.com/tutorials.htm) — Technical photography tutorials
- [freeCodeCamp — Graphic Design](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fZe67pxLGsc) — Graphic design fundamentals
- [Hack Design](https://hackdesign.org/) — Free design lessons curated by designers

### Photography & Design Channels
- [Jamie Windsor](https://www.youtube.com/c/JamieWindsor) — Composition, storytelling, and creative photography
- [Thomas Heaton](https://www.youtube.com/c/ThomasHeatonPhoto) — Landscape photography and process
- [Mango Street](https://www.youtube.com/c/MangoStreet) — Portrait photography and Lightroom editing
- [The Futur](https://www.youtube.com/c/thefutur) — Graphic design, branding, and business of design
- [Piximperfect](https://www.youtube.com/c/PiXimperfect) — Photoshop tutorials — clear and thorough

### Books & References
- [Understanding Exposure](https://www.amazon.com/Understanding-Exposure-Fourth-Photographs-Camera/dp/1607748509) — Bryan Peterson's photography classic
- [The Photographer's Eye](https://www.amazon.com/Photographers-Eye-Composition-Design-Digital/dp/0240809343) — Michael Freeman on composition
- [Steal Like an Artist](https://austinkleon.com/steal/) — Creative inspiration and finding your voice
- [Refactoring UI](https://www.refactoringui.com/) — Design for developers
