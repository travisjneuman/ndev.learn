## Coming Soon

The language of Apple's ecosystem. iOS apps, macOS applications, watchOS, tvOS, and server-side Swift (Vapor) — all with a modern, safe, and expressive language designed for performance.

This course covers Swift from fundamentals through SwiftUI and UIKit to building and shipping apps on the App Store.

## Top Resources to Get Started

### Official & Core Documentation
- [The Swift Programming Language](https://docs.swift.org/swift-book/) — Official Swift language guide covering every feature with examples
- [Apple Developer Documentation](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/) — Complete reference for SwiftUI, UIKit, and all Apple frameworks
- [Swift Standard Library](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/swift/swift-standard-library) — API reference for Swift's built-in types, protocols, and functions
- [Swift Playgrounds](https://www.apple.com/swift/playgrounds/) — Learn Swift interactively on iPad and Mac with guided lessons
- [Swift.org](https://www.swift.org/) — Official Swift open-source project with evolution proposals, community resources, and downloads
- [Apple Human Interface Guidelines](https://developer.apple.com/design/human-interface-guidelines/) — Design principles for building great Apple platform apps

### Interactive Courses & Platforms
- [Hacking with Swift — 100 Days of SwiftUI](https://www.hackingwithswift.com/100/swiftui) — Free daily projects building 19+ real apps, the most recommended free Swift course
- [Stanford CS193p](https://cs193p.sites.stanford.edu/) — Stanford's free iOS development course using SwiftUI, taught by Paul Hegarty
- [Exercism Swift Track](https://exercism.org/tracks/swift) — Mentored Swift exercises with community feedback
- [Kodeco (formerly Ray Wenderlich)](https://www.kodeco.com/) — Extensive tutorials, books, and video courses for Apple platforms
- [Hacking with Swift — Swift Playground](https://www.hackingwithswift.com/playgrounds) — Free coding challenges and experiments in Swift
- [Apple Education — Learn to Program with SwiftUI](https://education.apple.com/resource/250010591) — 90+ video lessons from Apple for building apps from scratch
- [Codecademy — Learn Swift](https://www.codecademy.com/learn/learn-swift) — Interactive beginner Swift course in the browser
- [Credo Academy](https://credo.academy/) — SwiftUI, SwiftData, and Apple AI courses
