## Coming Soon

The language of Apple's ecosystem. iOS apps, macOS applications, watchOS, tvOS, and server-side Swift (Vapor) — all with a modern, safe, and expressive language designed for performance.

This course covers Swift from fundamentals through SwiftUI and UIKit to building and shipping apps on the App Store.

## Top Resources to Get Started

### Official Documentation
- [The Swift Programming Language](https://docs.swift.org/swift-book/) — Official Swift language guide
- [Apple Developer Documentation](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/) — SwiftUI, UIKit, and frameworks
- [Swift Playgrounds](https://www.apple.com/swift/playgrounds/) — Learn Swift interactively on iPad/Mac

### Interactive Courses
- [Exercism Swift Track](https://exercism.org/tracks/swift) — Mentored Swift exercises
- [Hacking with Swift — 100 Days of SwiftUI](https://www.hackingwithswift.com/100/swiftui) — Free daily SwiftUI projects
- [Stanford CS193p](https://cs193p.sites.stanford.edu/) — Stanford's free iOS development course
- [Kodeco (formerly Ray Wenderlich)](https://www.kodeco.com/) — Tutorials and books for Apple platforms

### Video & Guided Learning
- [Sean Allen](https://www.youtube.com/c/SeanAllen) — Swift and iOS development tutorials
- [Paul Hudson — Hacking with Swift](https://www.youtube.com/c/PaulHudson) — SwiftUI and Swift content
- [Stewart Lynch](https://www.youtube.com/c/StewartLynch) — SwiftUI deep dives
- [Apple WWDC Videos](https://developer.apple.com/videos/) — Official sessions from Apple engineers

### Books
- [Swift Programming (Big Nerd Ranch)](https://bignerdranch.com/books/swift-programming/) — Comprehensive Swift guide
- [SwiftUI by Tutorials](https://www.kodeco.com/books/swiftui-by-tutorials) — Hands-on SwiftUI learning
