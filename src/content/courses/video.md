## Coming Soon

Tell stories through motion. Video production covers camera work, lighting, editing, color grading, and publishing — from smartphone content to cinematic productions.

This course builds from shooting fundamentals through DaVinci Resolve editing to color grading, motion graphics, and publishing workflows.

## Top Resources to Get Started

### Free & Open Source Tools
- [DaVinci Resolve (Free)](https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/products/davinciresolve) — Industry-standard editing, color grading, VFX (Fusion), and audio (Fairlight) in one app — the free version covers most professional needs
- [DaVinci Resolve Training](https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/products/davinciresolve/training) — Official Blackmagic training videos and certification program
- [Kdenlive](https://kdenlive.org/) — Open-source non-linear video editor with multi-track timeline, effects, and transitions for Linux, Windows, and macOS
- [Shotcut](https://shotcut.org/) — Free, open-source editor supporting 4K, hundreds of formats, and hardware encoding across all platforms
- [OpenShot](https://www.openshot.org/) — Beginner-friendly open-source editor with unlimited tracks, 3D titles, and keyframe animations
- [Blender Video Sequence Editor](https://www.blender.org/) — Blender's built-in non-linear editor with compositing nodes, motion tracking, and VFX — completely free and open source
- [Natron](https://natrongithub.github.io/) — Open-source compositing software modeled after Nuke, for node-based VFX and motion graphics
- [OpenCut](https://opencut.org/) — Open-source browser-based video editor with no downloads or installations required

### Books & Long-Form References
- [In the Blink of an Eye](https://www.amazon.com/Blink-Eye-Perspective-Film-Editing/dp/1879505622) — Walter Murch's classic on the art and theory of film editing
- [The Filmmaker's Handbook](https://www.amazon.com/Filmmakers-Handbook-Comprehensive-Digital-Age/dp/0452297281) — Comprehensive reference covering every aspect from scripting to distribution
- [Color Correction Handbook](https://www.amazon.com/Color-Correction-Handbook-Professional-Techniques/dp/0321929292) — Alexis Van Hurkman's definitive guide to professional color grading
- [Cinematography: Theory and Practice](https://www.amazon.com/Cinematography-Theory-Practice-Image-Filmmakers/dp/1138940925) — Blain Brown's textbook on camera technology, lens choice, lighting, and exposure
- [The Five C's of Cinematography](https://www.amazon.com/Five-Cinematography-Motion-Picture-Filming/dp/187950541X) — Joseph Mascelli's essential guide to camera angles, continuity, cutting, closeups, and composition
- [The Filmmaker's Eye](https://www.amazon.com/Filmmakers-Eye-Learning-Watch-Movies/dp/0240812174) — Gustavo Mercado on visual composition using frame analysis and diagrams
